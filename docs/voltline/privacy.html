Voltline does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your game progress stays on your device.

Data Collection

Voltline does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no personal information, no location data, no device identifiers, no advertising data.

Local Storage

Voltline stores your game progress (level completion, star ratings, and scores) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted anywhere and stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple handles your Game Center account and leaderboard data according to Apple's privacy policy.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Voltline does not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Voltline does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
