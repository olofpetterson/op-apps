Troubleshooting

🔈

No sound or music

Check that your device is not on silent mode and that the Music and SFX toggles in Settings are enabled. Voltline generates its own soundtrack — no external audio files are needed.

🔄

Purchase not showing

If your Full Game purchase isn't recognized, make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID. Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, contact us at the email below.

🕑

Performance issues

If the game feels slow, try enabling Reduced Motion in Settings. This simplifies visual effects while keeping gameplay unchanged. Closing other apps can also help on older devices.
