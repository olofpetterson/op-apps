Frequently Asked Questions

TechnoBuddy uses your microphone to capture audio in real-time. It analyzes onset patterns and frequency energy using FFT, then estimates tempo with Gaussian priors tuned to your selected genre.

TechnoBuddy includes presets for Techno (120-170 BPM), House (110-135 BPM), DnB (155-190 BPM), and Ambient (55-115 BPM). Each preset optimizes the detection range.

Troubleshooting

🎙

BPM not detecting

Make sure microphone access is granted in iOS Settings. Move closer to the audio source and ensure the correct genre preset is selected.

📈

BPM reading is doubled or halved

This can happen with certain rhythmic patterns. Try switching to a different genre preset that matches your music, as each preset has a tuned detection range.

🔈

Spectrum not showing

Verify that the app has microphone permission and that audio is playing. The spectrum visualizer requires an active audio signal to display.
